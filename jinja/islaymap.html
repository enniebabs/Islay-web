This web map visualizes places in Islay to help historical researchers understand past places better and create stories from comparing the distribution of features and their location which might be unbeknownst to them using the capabilities the GIS affords . It also contains qualititative information about such places like images associated and textual descriptions.

Information about family history in Islay and the places they lived is also shown.

The blue polygons that show on mouse over a designated cluster area display the extent to which the points (places) spread

Tne tabs on the rigght are used to extend the functionalities on the map

The data used to create this web map were acquired from Roger McWee's Islay Cultural Database

The icons used were made by Freepik from www.flaticon.com and is licensed by CC 3.0 BY
